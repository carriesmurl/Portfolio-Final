”The great gift of human beings is that we have the power of empathy, we can all sense a mysterious connection to each other.” —Meryl Streep

I'm Carrington - a Product designer from Austin. I enjoy creating user-centric, and delightful human experiences.

I′m finishing up my education at Austin Community college, majoring in UX/UI and Visual Design. My goal is to engage and inspire others to collaborate with one another in different ways, so that we may break the barriers of design.

I balance multiple jobs along with personal creative work. My skills range from sales and digital marketing to full stack front end development, but my most successful trait is to make others laugh. I am a true empath. In my free time I indulge in my creative hobbies such as stand-up comedy, modeling, music, painting, volleyball, and horseback-riding.

In my creative approach —no matter the medium— my goal is to find points of tension behind the idea I′ve been assigned or am interested in. I do several iterations on each point and usually end up refining the one I find most controversial.

For me, controversy isn′t about instigation, but rather, a way to grab your audience. It′s a way to plant a little bit of desire in their hearts; a yearning to turn the page and learn more about your idea. Ultimately, it′s about creating memorable images.
